Construction Services

New Construction

We offer a wide variety of construction services to meet the client needs. Whether renovating an existing building, construction of a new building, or installing a new roof, we have the project team to meet your goals.

Design Build

The design build method of project delivery is one in which the owner contracts with PLUS BUILDERS Construction Services as the sole entity responsible for the complete design and construction of the project. As a Design Builder, PB will work directly with the owner in regards to achieving his/her vision for the end-product. PB is responsible to assemble a team of qualified engineers that are best suited to translate the owner’s vision into a viable building design. After such a design is achieved, PB will be responsible for the sub-contractor contracting. Upon successful completion of permitting requirements, PB Construction Services directly administrates and manages all aspects of construction, which allows us to work towards consolidation of the project timelines as we move quickly towards project completion.

General Contracting

PB Construction Services provides a variety of General Contracting services throughout Rwanda. We are proud to provide general contracting services for all stages of construction from Pre-construction Services such as Project Scheduling, Estimating, Value Engineering, Constructability Reviews, Permitting and more. Our management services can provide overview of a project from start to finish including small and medium projects.

Construction Management

We work in the owner’s best interests through every phase of the project from conceptualization through final project turnover. PB does not act as a consultant to the owner, but performs the work of a general contractor during the construction phase. Our construction management services include scheduling, plan review, quality control assurance, new construction, building additions, building renovations, design-build, and tenant finish outs.
